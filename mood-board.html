Handmade Wooden Bench Seating

Rustic Log Benches (DIY)

  • Use 3 tree trunk sections as bases (12" diameter)
  • Lay a long plank or split log across the top
  • Seal with water-based polyurethane
  • Source: fallen trees, local sawmill, lumber yard scraps

Farmhouse Plank Benches

  • Simple 2x10 or 2x12 planks on X-frame legs
  • Sand smooth, stain in dark walnut or leave natural
  • 8ft bench seats ~4 guests

Cedar/Reclaimed Wood

  • Family-made from scrap cedar (meaningful + budget-friendly)
  • Can double as wedding favors for family members afterward

Bench Accessories

  • Cozy throws: Plaid or solid navy/burgundy blankets draped on bench ends for chilly evenings
  • Greenery sprigs: Tie small eucalyptus bundles to bench ends with twine
  • Programs: Place on each seat tied with copper ribbon

Artisanal Rug Aisle Runner

A collected, "purposefully mismatched" runner of vintage rugs layered down the aisle. Bohemian but grounded. Warm, worn-in textiles against the green grass.

Color Guidelines for Rugs

Look for rugs with ANY of these colors present:

  • Deep reds / burgundy / rust
  • Navy blue / indigo
  • Copper / terracotta / burnt orange
  • Cream / ivory backgrounds
  • Black accents

Quantity Needed

5-10 rugs depending on aisle length and rug sizes. Mix runners (2-3ft x 6-8ft) with smaller rectangles. Overlap rugs slightly. Vary sizes for organic feel. Secure edges with landscape staples if windy.

Tent & Rentals

  • Compare at least 3 rental companies

Food

Alcohol
